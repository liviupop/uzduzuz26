Photographer and creative practitioner with skills in visual storytelling, social media, and youth facilitation.

## role at uzinaduzina

Radu is responsible for photography and video documentation of uzinaduzina's projects, with particular attention to cultural events and youth-led activities. His visual work supports the dissemination of project outcomes and provides the archive of images that the organisation draws on for reports, websites, publications, and grant applications. He also supports youth-led creative outputs when projects call for it.

- [Belgica 100 Workshops](project-belgica-100-workshops) (2025): photographer
