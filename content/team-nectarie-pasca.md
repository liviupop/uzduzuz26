High school student studying astronomy and astrophysics. Within uzinaduzina, Nectarie is one of our main contacts when we need to assess the needs, interests, and expectations of young participants.

## role at uzinaduzina

Nectarie helps us stay close to the realities of the age group we most often work with. When we prepare youth activities, workshops, exchanges, or educational formats, he is one of the first people we turn to for an honest reading of what feels relevant, what feels forced, and what young people are actually curious about at a given moment.

His contribution is especially important in the early stages of programme design, when a project still exists more as an intention than as a finished format. He helps us test language, themes, and expectations before they harden into a plan.

## areas of interest

- Astronomy and astrophysics
- Youth learning needs and motivations
- Science communication for young audiences
- Educational activities that connect curiosity with real questions

## where Nectarie helps most

Nectarie is especially valuable when we need direct feedback from a high-school perspective: how young participants read a proposal, what kinds of activities feel meaningful to them, and which topics are likely to sustain attention beyond the first impression.
