High school student studying piano and classical music. Within uzinaduzina, Siluan is one of our main contacts when we need to understand the needs, rhythms, and interests of young people.

## role at uzinaduzina

Siluan contributes a perspective rooted in the everyday experience of a young person moving seriously through education and artistic training. That makes him especially useful when we are trying to judge whether a programme is genuinely engaging, whether its pace is realistic, and whether its language speaks to young participants without flattening their intelligence.

Alongside Nectarie, he helps us assess youth needs from inside the generation we are addressing. This kind of contact matters to us because we do not want to design educational or cultural activities only from the adult side of the table.

## areas of interest

- Piano and classical music
- Youth participation in cultural and educational programmes
- Assessing young people's expectations and barriers
- Artistic discipline, practice, and motivation

## where Siluan helps most

Siluan is especially helpful when we test whether an idea feels alive to young people or merely well-intentioned to adults. He helps us calibrate tone, difficulty, and relevance before activities reach participants.
