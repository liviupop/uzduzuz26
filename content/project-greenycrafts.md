June 2023 to May 2024. Erasmus+ KA152-YOU coordinated by 4 Elements (Croatia). uzinaduzina as partner. €17,602.

## what it was

An Erasmus+ youth project on cultural exchange, coordinated by our Croatian partners 4 Elements. The central theme: how to integrate traditional crafts into sustainable models of production for the future. Young people from several European countries worked together on reconnecting inherited manufacturing practices with the circular logic of sustainable economies.

## why it mattered

For us, the first structured collaboration with 4 Elements, the Croatian organisation with whom, two years later, we ran [Wooden Wonders](project-wooden-wonders) (2025). Greenycrafts opened the door.

Thematically, it confirmed an intuition that we have refined in other projects: **heritage is not in opposition to the future**; it is one of the few resources from which we can borrow vocabulary for building the future.

## what we did

- Youth exchanges on craft and sustainable production.
- Co-creating educational materials with the partners.
- Activities in partner communities.
