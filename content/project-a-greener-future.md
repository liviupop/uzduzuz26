March 2024. Five young volunteers from uzinaduzina took part in an exchange organised by our partners CCIF in Paphos, Cyprus. The first European activity of 2024.

## what it was

An Erasmus+ KA151 youth exchange organised by [CCIF](partner-ccif) (Cyprus), with uzinaduzina as beneficiary. Five young volunteers travelled to Paphos for a European-mobility experience focused on integrating sustainability values into youth activities.

## why it mattered

Three dimensions:

First, **exposure to the network**: for young volunteers, the first direct contact with a partner organisation in another country matters enormously. Not just for the project itself, but for the construction of practical European awareness.

Second, **continuity with CCIF**: the first of two Paphos mobilities in 2024, followed by [Echoes of Unity](project-echoes-of-unity) in November. CCIF remains our strategic Cypriot partner.

Third, **integration of sustainability values** into youth activities, in continuation of our work through [Green Genesis Startup](project-green-genesis-startup).

## what we did

- Travel of the five volunteers to Paphos.
- Activities structured by CCIF on the theme of sustainability.
- Cultural exchange with young people from other participating countries.

## numbers

- **Travel budget:** €1,545
- **Participants:** 5 uzinaduzina volunteers
- **Duration:** 6 days (2–7 March 2024)
- **Location:** Paphos, Cyprus
