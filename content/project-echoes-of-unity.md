November 2024. Erasmus+ KA151 mobility. Three youth workers from uzinaduzina at Paphos, Cyprus. Published output: *A Fresh Youth Worker’s Guide*.

## what it was

An Erasmus+ KA151 mobility in which three youth workers from uzinaduzina took part in activities organised by our partners [CCIF](partner-ccif) at Paphos, Cyprus, from 11 to 15 November 2024. The general theme: professional development for a sustainable future, with cultural exchange as the method of capacity-building.

## why it mattered

For us, a second step in consolidating the relationship with CCIF in 2024 (after [A Greener Future](project-a-greener-future) in March). Same host, different group from us, different working methods.

For the European youth-work sector, a concrete output: **A Fresh Youth Worker’s Guide**, produced as a result of the exchange. It is the kind of output that outlives the project itself.

## what we did

- Three youth workers from uzinaduzina travelled to Paphos.
- Activities structured by CCIF on the theme of cultural exchange.
- Co-creating educational materials.
- Dissemination in partner communities.

## output

**ECHOES OF UNITY: A Fresh Youth Worker’s Guide** — published as a result of the project.

## numbers

- **Mobility budget:** €423.5
- **Participants:** 3 youth workers
- **Duration:** 5 days
- **Location:** Paphos, Cyprus
