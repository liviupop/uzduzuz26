November 2023. Erasmus+ KA151-YOU. A professional-development activity in Cyprus, organised by CCIF.

## what it was

A five-day professional-development activity (21–25 November 2023) in Cyprus, with uzinaduzina as the beneficiary organisation. The theme: professional development for youth workers, focused on jointly modelling a shared European future.

## why it mattered

This mobility inaugurated a cycle of four projects with [CCIF](partner-ccif) across 2023–2025: Modeling the Future (2023), [A Greener Future](project-a-greener-future) (March 2024), [Echoes of Unity](project-echoes-of-unity) (November 2024), [AI4NGOs](project-ai4ngos) (August 2025). It is the moment when our relationship with CCIF moved from one-off cooperation ([Green Genesis Startup](project-green-genesis-startup)) into a steady rhythm of exchanges.
