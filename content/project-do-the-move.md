February 2020 to December 2021. Erasmus+ KA105 Youth Exchange. One of the first European projects in which we were beneficiaries, right through the pandemic.

## what it was

An Erasmus+ KA105 exchange on rural development, with our role as beneficiary organisation. The project crossed the most difficult period of the pandemic (2020–2021), which forced us to reorganise activities and taught us valuable lessons about adaptation.

## why it mattered

Three things.

First: this is one of the first documented European activities of uzinaduzina as an organisation. It built routes and relationships we used later.

Second: it set out, from the beginning, that **the rural matters to us** and that we did not intend to be only an urban Cluj-based organisation. Heritage, community, rootedness are rural themes before they are urban.

Third: it forced into us a set of pandemic-adaptation capabilities that we carried forward.

## numbers

- **Duration:** nearly 2 years (with pandemic extensions)
- **Role:** beneficiary
- **Lead:** organisation in Croatia
