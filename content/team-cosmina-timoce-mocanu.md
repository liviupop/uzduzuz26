Researcher with the Romanian Academy. Specialist in ethnology, oral history, and intangible cultural heritage. Within uzinaduzina, she contributes research grounding, methodology design, and evaluation of learning outcomes.

## role at uzinaduzina

Cosmina brings the academic depth of Romanian ethnology into uzinaduzina's projects. She designs methodologies for educational and cultural activities that engage with intangible heritage, develops fieldwork protocols, and contributes to the evaluation of learning outcomes. Her work ensures that projects working with folklore, oral history, and traditional practices do so with scholarly rigour rather than romanticised approximations.

## background

Cosmina is a Senior Researcher (CS II) at the Romanian Academy, Cluj-Napoca branch, currently affiliated with the *Sextil Pușcariu* Institute of Linguistics and Literary History. She previously worked at the *Folklore Archive of the Romanian Academy* institute (2010–2019). Her PhD, defended at Babeș-Bolyai University in 2012, examined funeral practices and commemoration of the dead in the polyphonic cultural space of Sulina, in the Danube Delta.

She has held postdoctoral positions at the West University of Timișoara and has been a researcher at *Vasile Alecsandri* University of Bacău. Her transnational mobility has taken her to the Hungarian Academy of Sciences in Budapest and to the Université Libre de Bruxelles. She is a member of the Romanian Ethnological Sciences Association (since 2010) and of the International Society for Ethnology and Folklore (since 2023). Between 2017 and 2021 she served on the National Commission for the Safeguarding of Intangible Cultural Heritage at the Romanian Ministry of Culture.

She currently directs the GAR 2023-029 research project *ADELIS*, on interwar ethnographic and linguistic documents on Romanian foodways, funded by the Romanian Academy through the *Patrimoniu* Foundation. She has also been principal researcher on FOODie (digital collection of Romanian food heritage) and a senior researcher on eCULTFOOD (digitisation of food heritage in the Bacău region).
